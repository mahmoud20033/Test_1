Q: What if something goes wrong?
A: Check the logs (heroku logs --tail), read troubleshooting guides.

Q: Can I update my app later?
A: Yes! Just push new code: git push heroku main

Q: Do I need a custom domain?
A: No, you can use the platform's free domain.

Q: Is HTTPS required?
A: Yes, all platforms provide free SSL automatically.

Q: What if I want to switch platforms?
A: You can! Your code doesn't change, just redeploy on the new platform.

Q: How do I add a custom domain?
A: Add DNS record pointing to your platform's domain.

Q: How do I backup my database?
A: MongoDB Atlas has automatic backups. Enable them in the console.

Q: Will my app go offline?
A: Unlikely. Cloud platforms are designed for 99.9% uptime.
